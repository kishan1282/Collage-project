About Us

Welcome to GreenLeaf Nursery, your trusted destination for fresh, healthy, and affordable plants. We specialize in indoor plants, outdoor plants, medicinal herbs, and flowering plants – all grown with care in our eco-friendly nursery.

Our mission is to bring nature closer to your home by offering premium-quality plants, safe paper packaging(which is ecofriendly), timely delivery, and excellent customer support.

With over 500+ happy customers across India, GreenLeaf Nursery continues to grow with love, trust, and passion for greenery.
